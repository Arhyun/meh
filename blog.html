Caravan Black

Located at Seven/NEO, 4th Ave, Taguig, 1634 Metro Manila Caravan Black Coffee Shop is known for its delicious coffee, friendly staff, and cozy atmosphere.

Customers often appreciate the variety of coffee options, from traditional espresso-based drinks to unique blends and pour-overs.

Tweedle Book Cafe

Tweedle Book Cafe is a unique coffee shop in Quezon City that combines a library with a coffee shop.

The atmosphere is cozy and inviting, with comfortable chairs and bookshelves lining the walls. The menu features a variety of coffee and tea options, as well as light snacks like pastries and sandwiches.
